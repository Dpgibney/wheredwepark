Frequently asked questions

How do I save where I parked?

Open a vehicle, then tap Save parking spot. The app saves your current location, or you can drop a pin on the map to park the car somewhere other than where you're standing. You can also add a note and a photo.

How do I find my car later?

Open the vehicle to see its saved spot on the map. Tap the location to open directions in Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze.

How does sharing a vehicle work?

From a vehicle you own, open its share screen and enter the email address of someone with an account. They'll see a pending invite on their home screen and must accept it before they can view the parking location. Either person can stop sharing at any time, which immediately removes access.

Why does the app ask for my location?

Location is used only while you're using the app, to save your parking spot and show your position on the map. It's never tracked in the background. You can decline the permission and still use the app by placing pins on the map manually.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account in the app. This permanently removes your account, vehicles, saved spots, notes, and photos. You can also email us to request deletion.

Why am I seeing ads?

The app is free and supported by banner ads from Google AdMob. Where required, you can adjust personalization in Settings → Ad Privacy Options. We never share your account, vehicle, or parking data with advertisers.
